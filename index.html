Features

#Feature 1

Projects

Stay Organized From Start to Finish: Manage All Your Projects in One Screen

Builder Pro keeps your construction projects under complete control. View ongoing projects, access completed ones, and effortlessly add new ventures to your portfolio. Our comprehensive project management system streamlines your workflow and ensures you stay on top of everything.

#Feature 2

Transactions

Take Control of Your Finances: Manage All Transactions in One Screen

Builder Pro simplifies your financial management. View all your construction project transactions seamlessly, including payments received, payments made, and reasons for each transaction. Gain instant insights into your cash flow with clear summaries of total income, expenses, and current balance displayed prominently in the app. Effortlessly add new transactions, whether it's a payment you receive from a client or a material purchase you make, keeping your financial records organized and up-to-date.

#Feature 3

Tasks

Boost Efficiency: Streamline Your Workflow with Powerful Task Management

Builder Pro empowers you to stay ahead of project deadlines with its robust task management system. Get a clear picture of your entire workflow at a glance, with ongoing, completed, and delayed tasks prominently displayed. Effortlessly add new tasks, assign them to team members, and track progress in real-time. Builder Pro keeps everyone connected and accountable, ensuring your construction projects run smoothly and efficiently.

#Feature 4

Material

Never Run Out Again: Take Control of Your Construction Materials

Builder Pro simplifies the way you handle your construction materials. Say goodbye to spreadsheets and wasted resources! Our comprehensive material management system keeps track of everything, from inventory levels to delivery schedules. Get real-time insights into what materials you have on hand, what needs to be ordered, and avoid costly delays due to material shortages. Optimize your material procurement process, minimize waste, and ensure your projects stay on budget.
